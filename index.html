About Us

Welcome to Vertex Autosolution, your trusted partner in sourcing quality vehicles through bank auctions. We specialize in helping individuals and businesses find affordable cars, SUVs, and commercial vehicles that come directly from banks and financial institutions. Our mission is simple: to make the process of buying bank auction vehicles transparent, reliable, and hassle-free. With years of experience in the automotive and auction industry, we provide end-to-end support — from vehicle listings and documentation guidance to smooth handovers.


Our Vision

To legalize and streamline vehicle auctions conducted by Banks and NBFCs, ensuring a trusted ecosystem for buyers, sellers, and financial institutions. We are redefining how repossessed and asset-auctioned vehicles reach genuine buyers — no middlemen, no hidden process, only transparency.
